### **2025 Q1 a). Define Artificial Intelligence? Have you ever used any AI technology like ChatGPT? Do you think that it has intelligence? If yes, explain in detail. (8 Marks)**

#### üìù High-Yield Exam Note (Write this for full marks)

**1. Definition of Artificial Intelligence (AI):**
Artificial Intelligence is a field of computer science aimed at building systems capable of performing tasks that require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. It can be categorized into four approaches:
*   Thinking Humanly
*   Thinking Rationally
*   Acting Humanly
*   Acting Rationally

**2. Personal Experience with AI (ChatGPT):**
Yes, I have used AI technology, specifically **ChatGPT**. I have used it for tasks like code debugging, summarizing complex articles, and brainstorming creative ideas. It serves as a highly efficient **Virtual Assistant.**

**3. Does ChatGPT have Intelligence?**
**Yes**, ChatGPT possesses "Narrow" or "Weak" Artificial Intelligence. It does not have consciousness, but it exhibits intelligence through:
*   **Contextual Understanding:** It "remembers" previous parts of a conversation to provide relevant answers.
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** It processes and generates human-like text by identifying patterns in massive datasets (LLM - Large Language Model).
*   **Problem Solving:** It can solve logical puzzles, write code, and explain complex theories.
*   **Adaptability:** It can change its tone (formal/informal) and format based on user instructions.

**Conclusion:** While it lacks a soul or real emotions, its ability to **process, learn from data, and provide rational responses** makes it an intelligent agent.
